# Human Rating Rubric (Phase 2 Calibration)

Scale each criterion from 1 (low) to 5 (high).

## Appropriateness
- 1: Off-task or violates constraints.
- 2: Weak relation to cue/context.
- 3: Partially aligned with cue/context.
- 4: Mostly aligned and coherent.
- 5: Fully aligned, coherent, and constraint-faithful.

## Usefulness (AUT only)
- 1: Not usable or unsafe.
- 2: Barely usable; unclear value.
- 3: Some practical value with caveats.
- 4: Clearly useful and feasible.
- 5: Highly useful, feasible, and context-aware.

## Notes
- Rate independently from language fluency.
- Penalize harm or impractical proposals.
- For CDAT, ignore style and rate semantic cue relevance.
